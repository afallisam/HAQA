About HAQA

Algeria's Most Advanced Private Hospital

The Hospital Algéro-Qatari-Allemand (HAQA) is a joint venture between Algeria's National Investment Fund (FNI) and Qatari investors — purpose-built to international standards in Sidi Abdallah, Algiers.

🏗️
Purpose-Built Greenfield Facility 60,000 m² designed from the ground up to JCI-aligned standards — every space engineered for clinical excellence and patient comfort.
🌍
International Partnership Developed by Algerian Qatari Healthcare Services Company (AQHCSC) and operated by an international management team with deep MENA expertise.
🚀
~950 Direct Jobs From Day One Join the founding team and help define the institution — clinical protocols, operational culture, and patient care standards — before opening day.
